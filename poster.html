Shall Make, Shall Be

Website Design 2020


A study and redesign of the “Shall Make, Shall Be: The Bill of Rights at Play,” website.The Frank-Ratchye STUDIO for Creative Inquiry at Carnegie Mellon University is hosting “Shall Make, Shall Be," an exhibition of playable works centered on the individual amendments in the US Bill of Rights.
